Our Story

Born in Aruba,
Built for Aruba

For over 10 years, Eco Cooling Solutions has been Aruba's go-to partner for air conditioning installation, maintenance, and repair. We started with a simple mission: deliver honest, high-quality HVAC service that island residents and businesses can truly rely on — and we haven't looked back since.

Aruba's tropical climate presents unique challenges. The constant heat, humidity, and salt-laden sea air are tough on any cooling system. That's why our technicians are trained specifically for these island conditions — from corrosion-resistant installation practices to service intervals engineered for tropical environments.

We are certified to install, service, and repair over 13 major AC brands, ensuring every customer gets the right solution for their home, villa, or commercial space. Quality craftsmanship and lasting results are the foundation of everything we do.
