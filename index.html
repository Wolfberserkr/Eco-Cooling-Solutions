About Us

Aruba's Trusted Cooling Specialists

For over a decade, Eco Cooling Solution Aruba has been keeping homes and businesses across Aruba comfortably cool. Founded right here on the island, we understand the unique challenges that our tropical climate and salty sea air present to air conditioning systems.

Our team of certified technicians is passionate about delivering top-tier service — from rapid repairs to expert installations — always using quality parts and proven methods that stand the test of time in the tropics.

  • Certified and fully insured technicians
  • Service for all major brands on the market
  • Island-wide coverage from north to south
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
