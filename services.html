Service 03

Maintenance & Service Plans

Regular maintenance is the single most effective way to extend your AC system's lifespan, prevent unexpected breakdowns, and keep energy costs under control. Dirty coils, clogged filters, and low refrigerant silently reduce efficiency and shorten equipment life.

Our structured service plans take the guesswork out of maintenance. We schedule visits at the right intervals, document system performance over time, and alert you to any developing issues before they turn into costly emergency repairs.

From single-unit homeowners to large commercial facilities, we have a maintenance plan sized to fit your property and your budget.

  • Scheduled maintenance visits
  • Filter cleaning and replacement
  • System performance check and report
  • Flexible plan options for every budget

Service 04

Anti-Corrosion Protective Coating

Aruba's tropical coastal environment is uniquely harsh on AC equipment. Salt-laden sea air accelerates corrosion on condenser coils and heat exchangers, dramatically reducing efficiency and cutting system lifespan well short of its potential.

Our specialized anti-corrosion coating creates a tough barrier between your AC coils and the elements, dramatically slowing the corrosion process. Units treated with our coating consistently outlast untreated systems by years — making this one of the highest-return investments you can make for your cooling equipment on the island.

Treatment is quick, non-invasive, and compatible with all brands and unit types. It can be applied to new installations or existing systems at any point in their life.

  • Specialized anti-salt marine coating
  • Extends system life up to 3x longer
  • Significantly reduces corrosion damage
  • Available for all unit types and brands
